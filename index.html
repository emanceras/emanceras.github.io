About me

I was born in Málaga, Spain. I studied in the high school of my neighborhood and when I finished Bachillerato when I was seventeen, I decided to study in Madrid, at URJC the first year of Tourism degree. After that, I came back to Málaga where I followed studying the degree. Afterwards, when I was twenty-one, I passed the entrance examination to the Army, belonging to “Anti-aircraft artillery”. I had to go to Cádiz and then to Zaragoza. After four years of hard work and far from my home, I decided to change it for what I really loved since I was a child: Technology. Since then, I have not stopped to study and training myself for this world. Nowadays I have specialized in Big Data and IA, and furthermore as RPA Developer. I am looking forward to continue learning and developing my knowledges, as part of this process at the end of the year, I will start the degree of Applied Data Science at UOC. In my spare time I do sports, especially running. I have participated in several competitions and it is something that I love. I also spend my free time traveling and photography. Finally, I want to say that a phrase which is referent for me in life is: “SIC PARVIS MAGNA” that means: “Greatness born from small beginnings”.
